ABSTRACT (A brief narrative summary of the content of the dataset):
Madagascar Region (Farita) Boundaries, Administrative Level 1.  
Cleaned & merged from the commune (adm3) shape data obtained from Madagascar BNGRC (National Disaster Management Office) in 2011, based on the Region name field, by OCHA.
Differences from SALB and GAUL Regional Boundaries: 
Toamasina II District falls in Atsinanana Region according  the commune shapefile, however it falls in Analanjirofo region in the SALB and GAUL region boundaries.

*There were previously 6 provinces (Faritany), which were larger than Regions, but the provinces were dissolved in 2007 and the regional boundaries became the first administrative boundary level.
